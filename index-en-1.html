Flight Controller

Independent R&D fixed-wing flight controller is customized for industrial fixed-wing UAVs. Flight controller realizes autonomous flight all the way, one-key launching, autonomous landing, greatly reducing operation complexity; high integration design makes the whole flight controller system safer and more reliable; high reliability industrial components as the hardware is tested to ensure product quality; flight controller has completed hundreds of successful flight validation which covers all major application scenarios, fully demonstrating the safety and stability of the product.

Ground Station

Customized for demands of various industries, the ground control station software combines flying route planning and flying operation with friendly interface, simple operation and powerful functions.

  • Easy Operation
  • Intelligent Planning
  • Simple Operation
  • The software is user-centered. Users can quickly grasp the basic functions of the software without understanding relevant professional knowledge to UAV, greatly reducing the threshold of UAV.
  • The software is equipped with intelligent route planning function. Ground control station software can automatically generate operating routes within a specified area based on the loading type and altitude of UAV, simplifying operation and greatly improving the efficiency of route planning.
  • Simple operating interface better monitors the operation process of the aircraft with flight attitude, signal strength and battery parameters in control. Flight route can be changed at any time under operation and one-key landing is supported when there is emergency.

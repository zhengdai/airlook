UAV OS FLIGHT CONTROLLER ABOUT US
AK1 AK2 AK3

AK2 is a low-cost flight controller designed for Oil-drive, medium-long range fixed-wing UAV with high reliability and high performance. Gesture, magnetic heading, static pressure, space velocity sensor, GPS receiver, digital radio and high integrated design are set in AK2 to ensure system reliable. Using PowerPC processor and real-time operating system, AK2 ensures software safety and reliable operation with higher anti-interference ability. Have been tested by hundreds of strict military flight, Ak2 is now widely used in surveying and mapping, investigation, monitoring, power of agriculture and forestry and environmental monitoring.

Simple Planning

According to job requirements and aircraft characteristics, landform map data can be automatic matched, the route can avoid obstacles, route planning can be quickly made in work zones and flight routes can be automatic generated.

Free Flight

Autonomous flight controller is supported with flight route being landed in advance to realize automatic launching, scheduled flights and automatic landing.

Integrated Design

Equipment number is reduced with highly integrated design, rich interface resources, attitude sensors, GPS, air pressure sensor, power management integration design to make flight controller system safer and more reliable.

Safe and Stable

Highly reliable industrial components, sensors, high heat and humidity, salt fog, extremely cold, hot test, wide voltage input, and the sensor information redundancy design are adopted to ensure a safe system and a stable flight.

Intelligent cloud management

Online cloud access is supported and flight controller parameters, flight status and flight data can be remote real-time viewed to monitor UAV operation.
